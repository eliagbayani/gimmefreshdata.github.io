Fresh Data - a Biodiversity Data Availability Monitor*

*A prototype.

The purpose of Fresh Data is to discover and follow records in biodiversity archives that match specific geospatial, temporal, taxonomic and trait constraints. Archives include, but are not limited to, GBIF, iDigBio and EOL TraitBank.

Visit our wiki for more information.
